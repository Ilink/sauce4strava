The concept of power profiling was created by Dr. Andy Coggan. It is a basic outline of the power requirements to be at a specific level of racing competitiveness. The levels are range from Cat 5 (lower) to World Class (the highest).

These rankings are meant to give you a rough idea of how your best power output would compare to the maximum capability of other racers, so they are most representative when you are working very hard.
